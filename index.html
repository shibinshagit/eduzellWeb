LEARNING PATH

Our program is divided into five phases.

  • PHASE 1

    Foundations

    Here, you'll get an introduction to the program and learning objectives, do a self-assessment, and learn the fundamentals of the subject matter.

  • PHASE 2

    Skill Building

    Here, you'll work on intermediate-level concepts and skills, do practice exercises and assessments, and work on individual and group projects with feedback and reflection.

  • PHASE 3

    Applied Learning

    Here, you'll work on real-world case studies and simulations, work on collaborative projects with an emphasis on practical application, and have group discussions and presentations.

  • PHASE 4

    Specialization

    Here, you'll get an in-depth exploration of specific topics or skills, work on hands-on projects with real-world applications, and get language training if applicable.

  • PHASE 5

    Capstone Project

    Here, you'll work on an independent project with mentorship and support, build your portfolio, and develop your career. You'll also give a final presentation and evaluation.
